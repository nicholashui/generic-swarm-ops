# Template — Critical Decision Interview

**Best for:** Rare / high-stakes calls  
**Output:** Decision Requirement Card (DRC)

## Interview prompts

1. What was the decision point?
2. What cues did you notice first?
3. What is the normal action vs exception paths?
4. What red flags force escalation?
5. What evidence is required before acting?

## Output path

- `business/experts/decision-requirement-cards/<drc_id>.json`
- Must validate against `business/schemas/decision-requirement-card.schema.json`
